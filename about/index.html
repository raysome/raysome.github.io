About Me

Dr. Rui Cao is currently a Research Assistant Professor at the Department of Land Surveying and Geo-Informatics (LSGI) and the Smart Cities Research Institute (SCRI), The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. He leads the Responsible Urban Intelligence Research Group. His research interests lie in Geospatial Artificial Intelligence (GeoAI) and Urban Informatics. He has published over 20 SCI/SSCI papers in venues related to Geographic Information Science. His works have been cited over 850 times according to Google Scholar. He has led and participated in several research grants as roles of PI and Co-I funded by NSFC, Microsoft, Tencent, etc. He also undertakes teaching and postgraduate training related to Geographic Information Science. He is a member of ACM SIGSPATIAL, CCF, CPGIS, IEEE, ISPRS, and ISUI, and he has served as a reviewer for NSFC and more than 20 renowned international journals.

The information of our group members can be found here. We invite motivated students and researchers to join, please contact Dr. Rui Cao for inquiry.
