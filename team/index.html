Research Group

The RUI (Responsible Urban Intelligence) Lab focuses on the research of GeoAI and Urban Informatics based on multisource and multimodal spatiotemporal big data. Our ultimate goal is using geospatial and AI technologies responsibly to help build sustainable and human-centred smart cities for the benefit of society.
