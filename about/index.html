Research group

The RUI Research Group invites outstanding students and researchers to join, please contact Dr. Cao for inquiry.

The Responsible Urban Intelligence (RUI) Research Group focuses on the research of urban informatics and responsible AI applied in the geospatial domain. Our ultimate goal is using artificial intelligence responsibly to help build human-centred smart cities for the benefit of society. The group leader is Dr. Rui Cao, currently a Research Assistant Professor at the Department of Land Surveying and Geo-Informatics (LSGI) and the Smart Cities Research Institute (SCRI), The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong SAR, China.
