About me

Hi, this is Ray. Welcome to my personal website!

I am currently a Research Assistant Professor at the Department of Land Surveying and Geo-Informatics (LSGI) and the Smart Cities Research Institute (SCRI), The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong SAR, China. I received my PhD degree from the University of Nottingham, UK and MEng and BEng degrees from Wuhan University, China. My research interests lie in urban informatics and applied artificial intelligence.

Just like the motto of UoN says, “sapientia urbs conditur” – a city is built on wisdom. I changed it a little bit – a smart city is built on collective wisdom, and I would like to do my part to help. Welcome aboard!
