Research Group

The Responsible Urban Intelligence (RUI) Lab focuses on the research of GeoAI and Urban Informatics based on multisource and multimodal data. Our ultimate goal is using geospatial and AI technologies responsibly to help build sustainable and human-centred smart cities for the benefit of society.

  • We invite motivated students and researchers to join us, please contact Dr. Rui Cao for inquiry.
